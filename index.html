Education

I am currently pursuing advanced courses in Computer Science at Scaler School of Technology,Bangalore building a strong foundation in modern software engineering and problem-solving.

Alongside, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, one of India’s most reputed institutions.That has helped me build a strong technical foundation and an analytical mindset.

My academic journey began at Kairali School, Ranchi, where I secured 98.4% in Class 10 and 95.2% in Class 12, earning the distinction of being the school topper in both. Throughout school, I consistently excelled in academics, winning multiple Science Olympiads which strengthened my curiosity and love for problem-solving. Beyond academics, serving in various leadership roles, including House Captain and Class Representative , leading teams and managing events that nurtured my leadership, teamwork, and communication skills. I also volunteered in numerous school events, developing a sense of responsibility, discipline, and adaptability. These formative years shaped me into a resilient and self-driven individual, instilling values that continue to guide my approach to both technology and life. They remain the foundation of my determination to keep learning, building, and creating meaningful impact.
